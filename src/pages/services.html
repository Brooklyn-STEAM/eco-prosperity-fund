What Type of Buisness are we ?


EcoProsperity is a service-based non-profit organization that focuses on providing educational programs and financial literacy services to low-income communities.

EcoProsperity excels at providing accessible, high-quality financial literacy education to low-income individuals, empowering them with the knowledge and tools needed to achieve financial independence.

We bring a unique blend of expertise and commitment to this initiative. With a strong background in finance and education, we possess the knowledge and skills necessary to design and deliver effective financial literacy programs. Moreover, our team consists of certified coders, with expertise in HTML as well as CSS to make functional and appealing websites which we will in order to benefit our business.




What are we Providing

There is a significant gap in financial literacy, particularly among low-income populations, which limits opportunities for upward economic mobility. By providing financial education and practical tools, EcoProsperity sees a unique opportunity to help these individuals break the cycle of poverty. One unit is defined as a completed financial education course or workshop, where participants learn core financial skills such as budgeting, saving, managing debt, and investing. Each course is designed to be delivered both online and in-person, reaching low-income communities through partnerships with local organizations and outreach efforts.


Help us , Help you !

Here at EcoProsperity, we are deeply committed to empowering communities through education and opportunity. We believe that knowledge is one of the most powerful tools for breaking cycles of poverty and creating long-term sustainability. That's why we encourage all our members to actively share what they learn as students at the Brooklyn Steam Center with individuals and families who may not have had the same access to resources or quality education.


At EcoProsperity, our mission goes beyond personal growth. We aim to foster a culture of generosity, collaboration, and empowerment — where education serves as a bridge toward equity and long-term prosperity for all.


We focus on redistributing this knowledge to underserved populations — including low-income families, underfunded schools, and individuals who are eager to improve their financial well-being. Our outreach includes workshops, mentorship programs, and community events aimed at building practical skills in technology, sustainability, and financial literacy.
