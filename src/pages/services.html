What Type of business are we ?



EcoProsperity is a service-based non-profit organization that focuses on providing educational programs and financial literacy services to low-income communities.

EcoProsperity excels at providing accessible, high-quality financial literacy education to low-income individuals, empowering them with the knowledge and tools needed to achieve financial independence.

We bring a unique blend of expertise and commitment to this initiative. With a strong background in finance and education, we possess the knowledge and skills necessary to design and deliver effective financial literacy programs. Moreover, our team consists of certified coders, with expertise in HTML as well as CSS to make functional and appealing websites which we will in order to benefit our business.




What are we Providing


There is a significant gap in financial literacy, particularly among low-income populations, which limits opportunities for upward economic mobility. By providing financial education and practical tools, EcoProsperity sees a unique opportunity to help these individuals break the cycle of poverty.

The unit is defined as a completed financial education course or workshop, where participants learn core financial skills such as budgeting, saving, managing debt, and investing. Each course is designed to be delivered both online and in-person, reaching low-income communities through partnerships with local organizations and outreach efforts.
