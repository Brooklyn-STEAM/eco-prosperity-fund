HOW WE PLAN TO HELP

Our business is called eco prosperity fund, EPF for short. Our goal is to help low income indiviuals with investing in things that will benefit them in the future. Now how we execute this plan is teaching those indiviuals financial literacy which is key when managing money. After they finish their full course we use the donantions to give a rough estimate of $100 so they can grow that amount by hundreds or even thousands. The point of our business is growth, we want to see people win.

MEET THE TEAM!

The team contains of 4 fullstack development students from the Brooklyn STEAM center. As a team this plan was decided on because we realized that there are a lot of indiviuals that don't know how to invest or just use their money properly because they weren't given the chance to learning financial literacy. Us students want to spread our knowledge in hopes of people gaining from it.
